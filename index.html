EatSmart is a service that makes choosing healthier food options, not just easier, but a joy. Plus, it will track how you are changing your diet over time, how that is affecting your goals and what you can do to level up your health without leveling down your dining experiences.




Making healthier choices will be as simple as checking in on the app wherever you are eating and either selecting a food item from the apps recommended food menu or choosing something not on the recommended menu in order to get healthier recommendations. Once you make your selection your choices will inform the types of recommendations made to you in the future. Plus, the nutritional information will be compiled to give yo a view of how the choices you've made might be impacting your overall health.

FAQ

What platforms will you support?
We will have both iOS and Android apps. You will also view your account data via the web.
Will you sell my data to third parties?
Never. The data is used to provide you with healthier options and to enable you to track your health. Your choices might help to target your ads, but that information will not be shared with advertisers or other interested parties.
